FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is offlog?

offlog is a mood journaling app that helps you track and visualize your daily emotions. Instead of long diary entries, you simply pick an emoji and a few keywords, and offlog turns them into a beautiful star map.

How long does it take to record an entry?

As little as 15 seconds. In Ultra-light mode, you just pick an emoji and optionally add a 10-second voice note. You can always choose a more detailed mode if you want to write more.

What is the Star Map?

The Star Map is a visual constellation of your emotions and keywords. Each keyword becomes a star, and connections form between related entries. Over time, you can see which topics and feelings are most central to your life.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. Your journal entries are stored securely and are never shared with third parties. You have full control over your data. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

When will offlog be available?

offlog is currently in beta. We're working hard to bring it to iOS and Android soon. Stay tuned for launch updates!

Is offlog free to use?

offlog will offer a free tier with core features. Premium features for deeper insights and customization will be available through a subscription.
